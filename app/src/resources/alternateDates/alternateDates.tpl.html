Alternate Dates

Technology to make you agile

Assisting your budget conscious and date flexible customer is now quick and efficient using the Bargain Finder Max Alternate Date Shopping API. Using a single query, search for multiple date pairs and return more itinerary options with each date pair. With +/- 1, +/-3 and +/-7 day options, you are sure to quickly return the best itinerary and lowest fare options to accommodate your customer.
