Inspirational Services

Technology to inspire

Inspire your customer with booking ideas for the perfect destination. The Inspirational APIs enable you to server your customer on a more personal level. Customers identify preferences such as activity, experience or geographical region, the API then returns top destinations to prompt their decision making transitioning from look to book. These API’s also enable you to create campaigns and packages based on destination and low fare pairing to driving booking revenue.
