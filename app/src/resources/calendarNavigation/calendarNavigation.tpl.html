Calendar Navigation

Technology to accommodate

Make your agency the go to resource to find the best itinerary option by referencing city pair data using the Lead Price Calendar or Advanced Calendar Search APIs. These Sabre options provide up to 192 days of shopped fare data for nonstop and overall lead price. The Advanced Calendar Search API will also provide the corresponding flight itinerary and fare breakdown. Using city pairs and including multiple parameters, create filters to support requirements and identify the least expensive, top itinerary options across your customers’ date range.
