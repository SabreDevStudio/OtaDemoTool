Resources

Sabre offers extensive air shopping APIs to support your business needs. These API demo widgets offer quick access to a wealth of information that will enable customization and customer support.

Basic Search

Grow your business and expand your search capabilities. Drive your bookings by providing not just the best price, but the best option at the best price using the Bargain Finder Max API.

Calendar Navigation

Enhance your customer experience by returning the cheapest fares for an extended departure timeframe, such as all departures days within the next three months.

Alternative Dates

Quickly and efficiently identify itinerary options for the flexible travel customer. Provide multiple itineraries with the cheapest fares across varied date pairs to find the best option.

Inspirational Services

Provide more than an itinerary, inspire your customer to find the trip they always wanted with ideas tailored to their favorite activity or destination.

Intelligence Services

Offer travel trend insights to help your customer find the right trip for them; from seasonality to purchase decision, create an itinerary you are sure to sell.

Compare

Identify the features which best fit your business needs using the compare widget. Run two searches side by side and know you have implemented the right solution.
